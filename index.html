“Everybody should learn to program a computer, because it teaches you how to think" Steve Jobs-1995

That phrase was on a wall in the University, and also was my first phrase related to programming that I have kept since I read it. I didn’t know what will happen in my first quarter or even in my first class, but trust me when you get into this world you will never want to go out.

I love programming, but my favorite part is “the solution to any problem”. And here’s why, we have many different ways to solve only one problem. That’s why you will never stop learning.

I was not a creative person, but when I started here, this makes me a person who thinks in different ways or even how I see my problems differently than before. I already understand that my problems have more than only 1 solution. I just need to breathe and think about the best way to solve them!

For those and other reasons, I am here, writing this, to learn, to make my best, and to be the best programmer that I can be.
